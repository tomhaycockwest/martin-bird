About Martin Louis Bird

MLB Carpentry was established in 2009, working in the West London area I have built up a steady business operating purely on word of mouth and recommendations from repeat customers.

I studied at Acton West London College where I completed my three year apprenticeship working for a high end construction company and gained a NVQ Level 2 and NVQ Level 3 in site carpentry, in which I finished with a credit and a distinction and was named apprentice of the year. During this time I was also asked to represent the college in Joinery competitions at regional level where I came first two years in a row and progressed to compete at National level.

Over the last 6 and a half years I have gained a huge amount of experience working on projects from Loft conversions to custom made fitted wardrobes - this experience allows me to work alongside my customers giving them the best advice whilst making sure that I stick to the specification that they have given me.

I cover all aspects of carpentry and am able to take on and project manage a job from start to finish, from fitting out a full kitchen to hanging a door - I treat every job the same ensuring the highest quality of finish.

If you have any questions or would like some advice please do not hesitate to get in contact, I look forward to hearing from you.
